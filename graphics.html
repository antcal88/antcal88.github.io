Evaluation of web graphics

Site evaluated: LingsCars

Purpose of graphics on this site:

The graphics on the website are, obviously, overwhelming. It's so overwhelming that it's impossible to actually take the site seriously. Not only that, it's painful enough just to look on the site. The images are all super small so loading times aren't too bad, but it's still noticiable.

Guidelines for effective use of graphics

  1. No - Graphics are spilled around on the page.
  2. No - Graphics are placed on the page without any purpose.
  3. No - Graphics are large and overwhelming, and take your attention from the info.
  4. No - The site is generally painful to look at.
  5. No - Accessibility for disabled people is nearly impossible with all the flashing images.
